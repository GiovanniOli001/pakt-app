# PAKT Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Free Trial

PAKT offers a **7-day free trial** with full access to all features. This trial period allows you to thoroughly evaluate the App before making a purchase decision.

## Purchase Policy

Due to the generous free trial period, **all purchases are final and non-refundable**.

**Price:** $3.99 AUD (one-time payment, lifetime access)

## Exceptions

We will provide a full refund in the following circumstances:

### Technical Failure
If a bug, error, or technical issue with the App causes **permanent inability to access or use the service**, and we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe (14 days), you are entitled to a full refund.

### Duplicate Purchase
If you accidentally purchased the App multiple times, we will refund the duplicate transactions.

### Unauthorised Purchase
If a purchase was made without your authorisation (e.g., by a child), contact us within 48 hours of the transaction.

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund under the above exceptions:

1. **Email:** support@getpakt.app
2. **Include:**
   - Your purchase email address
   - Date of purchase
   - Description of the issue
   - Any relevant screenshots or error messages

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days.

## Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under Australian Consumer Law. If the App has a major failure or cannot be fixed, you may be entitled to a refund regardless of this policy.

A major failure includes when:
- The App is significantly different from its description
- The App is substantially unfit for its intended purpose
- The App has a defect that would have stopped you from buying it if you had known

## Contact Us

For refund enquiries or purchase issues:

**Email:** support@getpakt.app
