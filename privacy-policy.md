# PAKT Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

PAKT ("the App") is a family lunchbox and chore planning app created by an independent Australian developer. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Children's profiles:** Names, ages, and avatars you create for your children
- **Meal plans:** Lunchbox selections and food preferences
- **Chore data:** Chores assigned, completion status, and points earned
- **Email address:** Only if you purchase a license

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Analytics data:** We use Google Analytics to understand how the App is used. This includes:
  - Pages viewed and features used
  - Session duration
  - General location (city-level, not precise)
  - Device type and browser
- **License verification:** Device identifier for license validation

## Where Your Data Is Stored

**On your device:** All personal data (children's profiles, meal plans, chores, points) is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to clear it.

**On our servers:** We only store license information (email, license key, device ID) for customers who purchase the App.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Analytics:** Used for anonymous usage statistics. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Stripe:** Processes payments securely. See [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

PAKT is designed for parents to manage family activities. While parents may enter children's names and ages, this information is stored only on the parent's device and is not transmitted to us.

## Data Retention

- **Local data:** Remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the App
- **License data:** Retained for the duration of your license plus any period required by law
- **Analytics data:** Retained by Google Analytics per their standard retention policies

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete your data** anytime using the "Clear All Data" option in the App
- **Request deletion** of your license information by contacting us
- **Opt out of analytics** by using browser privacy settings or extensions

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure payment processing via Stripe
- No storage of payment card details

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the App.
